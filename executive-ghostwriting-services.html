Frequently Asked Questions

How do you capture and maintain my authentic voice in ghostwritten content?

We begin every project with comprehensive interviews to understand your communication style, perspective, and expertise. We analyze your existing content and speaking patterns to create a voice profile, then iteratively refine content to ensure it authentically represents how you think and communicate.

What level of involvement is required from me during the ghostwriting process?

We design our process to minimize your time commitment while ensuring quality results. Typically, you'll participate in 2-3 strategic interviews, provide periodic feedback on drafts, and approve final content. Most executives spend 10-15 hours total on a major project like a book.

Can you help with getting content published in major business publications?

Yes, we have established relationships with editors at major business publications and can help pitch and place your thought leadership content. Our publication success rate is significantly higher than individual submissions because we understand editorial requirements and timing.

How do you ensure confidentiality and discretion in the ghostwriting process?

We maintain strict confidentiality throughout the process and sign comprehensive NDAs. Our ghostwriters are experienced professionals who understand executive discretion requirements. We also provide guidance on what information to include or exclude based on competitive and regulatory considerations.

What's the difference between your book ghostwriting and article services?

Book ghostwriting is comprehensive authority building requiring 3-6 months and extensive research. Article services are strategic thought leadership pieces that can be completed in 2-4 weeks. Many executives start with articles to establish thought leadership before committing to book projects.

How do you measure the success of thought leadership content?

We track multiple success metrics including media coverage, speaking invitations, LinkedIn engagement, business development opportunities, and industry recognition. We provide quarterly reports showing how thought leadership content contributes to your professional objectives.

Can you help with both traditional publishing and self-publishing?

Yes, we support both paths. We can develop professional proposals for traditional publishers or manage the complete self-publishing process. We help you evaluate which approach best serves your goals based on timeline, control preferences, and marketing objectives.

Do you provide ongoing support after content is published?

Absolutely. We offer ongoing thought leadership support including content amplification, media relations, speaking opportunity development, and strategic content planning. Many clients prefer retainer relationships for continuous thought leadership development.

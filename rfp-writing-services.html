Frequently Asked Questions

How do you achieve a 78% win rate for RFP responses?

Our high win rate results from systematic opportunity analysis, competitive intelligence gathering, strategic positioning, and rigorous compliance processes. We only pursue winnable opportunities and develop strategies that leverage client strengths while addressing evaluator priorities and concerns.

What information do you need from us to develop a winning proposal?

We need access to key personnel for interviews, relevant past performance information, technical capabilities documentation, and pricing guidance. Our structured discovery process efficiently gathers essential information while minimizing time demands on your team.

How do you handle tight RFP deadlines and rush projects?

We maintain capacity for expedited projects and have developed streamlined processes for tight deadlines. Our expedited approach includes dedicated writer assignment, accelerated review cycles, and extended coverage to ensure quality delivery within compressed timeframes.

Do you have experience with our specific industry or procurement type?

We have extensive experience across federal, state, local, and commercial procurement in industries including defense, technology, healthcare, professional services, and infrastructure. Each project is staffed with writers who have relevant industry and procurement experience.

How do you ensure complete compliance with RFP requirements?

We use sophisticated compliance tracking systems and multi-tiered review processes to ensure every requirement is addressed. Our compliance matrix maps all requirements to specific responses, and we conduct multiple compliance verification reviews before final submission.

Can you help with teaming arrangements and partner coordination?

Yes, we have extensive experience coordinating multi-partner proposals and can facilitate teaming arrangements. We help develop partnership agreements, coordinate content contributions, and manage the complex stakeholder engagement required for successful team proposals.

What support do you provide after proposal submission?

We provide post-submission support including debriefing analysis, lessons learned development, and strategy for future opportunities. Many clients continue working with us for ongoing business development support and relationship management.

How do you protect confidential business information during the process?
